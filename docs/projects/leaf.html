LEAF creative arts

We have supported the work of Leaf Creative Arts by producing some how to videos for participants of the Moving memories cafe during lockdown.

These instructional videos have included activities such as drawing mandala, mindfull nature walks and making a garden pond.

We also supported Leaf creative arts with a recent Sensory Walk at Moseley Park for people living with dementia, their carers and families.

Providing access to therapeutic nature based activities in lockdown has supported participants and careers connection with the outdoor and nature and with their wellbeing.

If you are interested in supporting people living with dementia through nature engagement click Hire us link!
